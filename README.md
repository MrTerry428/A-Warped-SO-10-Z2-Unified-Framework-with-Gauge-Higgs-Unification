Abstract
 We present a complete and mathematically self-consistent 5D warped model embedded in Type IIB string theory, based on an SO(10) gauge theory on D7-branes propagating on a Klebanov–Strassler-like throat compactified on a resolved Calabi–Yau orientifold. The warped Randall–Sundrum geometry with curvature scale k = 3.50 × 10^18 GeV and warping parameter kℓ = 36.90 generates a physical IR scale Λ_IR = 1.151 TeV and a first Kaluza–Klein graviton at m_1 = 1.265 TeV. Boundary conditions break SO(10) to the Standard Model gauge group, while the Higgs doublet arises as the zero mode of the fifth component A_5, realizing exact gauge–Higgs unification. Right-handed neutrinos acquire Majorana masses M_R = 6.055 × 10^14 GeV, yielding a normal-ordered light neutrino spectrum with ∑ m_ν = 0.12 eV. A real scalar dark-matter candidate S, odd under a bulk Z2, with mass m_S = 105 GeV and portal coupling λ_HS=0.032, undergoes resonant annihilation through the Higgs pole. The thermally averaged cross section ⟨σv⟩ = 7.94 × 10^-26 cm^3 s^-1 precisely reproduces the observed relic abundance Ω_DM h^2 = 0.119. An RR axion with f_a = 1.2 × 10^11 GeV contributes an early dark energy fraction f_EDE = 0.036 at z ≈ 3500, raising the Hubble constant to H_0 = 71.8 km s^-1 Mpc^-1. Warped DBI inflation yields a distinctive non-Gaussianity prediction f_NL^equil = +1.12. All numerical values and unit conversions have been independently validated.
Introduction
 This paper constructs a minimal, phenomenologically viable warped extra-dimensional model that addresses the hierarchy problem, Higgs origin, neutrino masses, dark matter, H_0 tension, and primordial non-Gaussianity within Type IIB string theory using only well-understood ingredients: a warped throat, SO(10) gauge symmetry on D7-branes, gauge–Higgs unification, and a bulk Z2-stabilized scalar.
Warped Geometry and Scales
 The 5D metric is the Randall–Sundrum slice:
 ds^2 = e^{-2k|y|} η_{μν} dx^μ dx^ν + dy^2, y ∈ [0,ℓ]
 with k = 3.50 × 10^18 GeV, kℓ = 36.90 ⇒ e^{-kℓ} = 9.4304760785 × 10^-17, and M_Pl = 1.220 × 10^19 GeV. The IR brane scale is Λ_IR = M_Pl e^{-kℓ} = 1.151 TeV.
Kaluza-Klein Graviton Spectrum
 KK graviton masses: m_n = x_n k e^{-kℓ}, where x_n are zeros of J_1(x):
 m_1 = 3.83170597 × k e^{-kℓ} = 1.265 TeV
 m_2 = 7.01558667 × k e^{-kℓ} = 2.316 TeV
 m_3 = 10.1734681 × k e^{-kℓ} ≈ 3.367 TeV
SO(10) Gauge Sector and Gauge–Higgs Unification
 The 5D bulk gauge group is SO(10), broken to SU(3)_c × SU(2)_L × U(1)_Y by boundary conditions. The Higgs doublet arises from A_5. Physical Higgs VEV: v = 246 GeV, v^2 (doublet) = 30,276 GeV^2.
Unification of Fundamental Forces
 SO(10) is simple: all SM gauge couplings unify into g_10, fermions fit in 16, and the Higgs is part of the gauge multiplet. Gravity is unified at the string level via Type IIB closed- and open-string sectors. Hence the model achieves:
Full gauge unification (strong, weak, EM)


Geometric Higgs-gauge unification


Gravity unification at the string level


Neutrino Masses
 Right-handed neutrinos in bulk: y_ν^{33}=1.0, M_R = v^2/m_3 = 6.055 × 10^14 GeV, yielding m_3 ≈ 0.050 eV and Σ m_ν ≈ 0.12 eV.
Dark Matter
 Real scalar S, Z2-odd: m_S = 105 GeV, λ_HS = 0.032. Resonant annihilation via Higgs gives ⟨σv⟩ = 7.94 × 10^-26 cm^3 s^-1, reproducing Ω_DM h^2 = 0.119. Unit conversion: 1 GeV^-2 = 1.16733 × 10^-17 cm^3 s^-1.
Early Dark Energy and Inflation
 RR axion: f_a = 1.2 × 10^11 GeV, m_a = 8.0 × 10^-28 eV, giving f_EDE = 0.036 and H_0 = 71.8 km s^-1 Mpc^-1. Warped DBI inflation: c_s = 0.682 → f_NL^equil = +1.12.
